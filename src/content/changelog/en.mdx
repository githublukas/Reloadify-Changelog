## Newsletter sending banner {{ date: '2026-02-23T00:00Z' }}

Previously, once you sent a newsletter the only indication was a brief popup and a status label. After closing that popup there was no way to track the actual sending progress, leaving you wondering: "Is my newsletter actually being sent?" Now, every newsletter that is being sent or is scheduled shows a real-time progress banner in the newsletter overview, giving you full visibility into the delivery process. Once sending completes, the banner disappears automatically. Scheduled sends show the planned moment, and A/B tests display which phase is active - collecting test results or sending the winning variant.

### <SparkleIcon /> Improvements

- Added real-time sending progress banner in the newsletter overview
- Scheduled newsletters now show the planned send moment in the banner
- A/B test banners display the current phase (test collection vs. winner send)
- Banner automatically disappears once sending is complete
