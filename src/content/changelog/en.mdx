## Optimal send time {{ date: '2026-03-16T00:00Z' }}

Choosing the right moment to send your newsletter used to be guesswork. Now, Reloadify calculates the optimal send time for you. Based on historical performance data from your shop's previously sent newsletters, the system analyzes which hour of the day generates the best results. - Works with spread sending and A/B testing
- The summary shows exactly when the optimized newsletter will be sent

## Newsletter sending banner {{ date: '2026-02-23T00:00Z' }}

Previously, once you sent a newsletter the only indication was a brief popup and a status label. After closing that popup there was no way to track the actual sending progress, leaving you wondering: "Is my newsletter actually being sent?" Now, every newsletter that is being sent or is scheduled shows a real-time progress banner in the newsletter overview, giving you full visibility into the delivery process. Once sending completes, the banner disappears automatically. Scheduled sends show the planned moment, and A/B tests display which phase is active - collecting test results or sending the winning variant.

### <SparkleIcon /> Improvements

- Added real-time sending progress banner in the newsletter overview
- Scheduled newsletters now show the planned send moment in the banner
- A/B test banners display the current phase (test collection vs. winner send)
- Banner automatically disappears once sending is complete

## Dynamic product feeds, image resizing & more {{ date: '2026-01-12T00:00Z' }}

A packed update to start the new year. Dynamic product feeds let you add personalized content to your emails based on actual customer behavior - first ordered, last ordered, all ordered, or cross-sell products. No more fiddling with liquid code. On top of that, product images now resize automatically for a consistent look in every inbox, you can see segment memberships directly on each profile page, and the new "Test as" feature lets you preview any newsletter through the eyes of a specific customer.

### <SparkleIcon /> Improvements

- Dynamic product feeds with support for first, last, all ordered, and cross-sell products
- Smart cross-sell fallback chain: order history, then cart, then your custom fallback
- Automatic `main_image` resizing for consistent email layouts across all inboxes
- Custom image dimensions via liquid: `main_image | resize: 600, 300`
- Segment membership now visible directly on customer profile pages
- "Test as" functionality: preview newsletters as a specific customer by entering their email
